Overview

  • System: 25MW / 100MWh Battery Energy Storage System (BESS).
  • Description: ~30% design process phase — focused on safe, standards-driven system design and documentation.
  • Deliverables: CAD layout, ETAP one-line/component layout, and studies (cable thermal + arc-flash / electrical safety analyses).

My Role

  • Created the CAD drawing and physical/component layout.
  • Laid out components and system representation in ETAP.
  • Performed ETAP studies including cable thermal checks and electrical safety analyses (e.g., arc-flash).

Standards & Safety

Design Decisions

  • Selected eco-friendly transformer oil to reduce environmental impact in the event of leaks.
  • Oversized cables to improve thermal margin and reduce overheating risk.
  • Reviewed and compared spec sheets to select key components (transformers, capacitors, fuses, batteries, inverters).
